Essential Math Formulas

Comprehensive collection of formulas for Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, and Calculus

Algebra Formulas

  • Quadratic Formula: x = [-b ± √(b²-4ac)] / 2a
  • Slope Formula: m = (y₂ - y₁)/(x₂ - x₁)
  • Distance Formula: d = √[(x₂ - x₁)² + (y₂ - y₁)²]
  • Exponent Rules: aᵐ × aⁿ = aᵐ⁺ⁿ, (aᵐ)ⁿ = aᵐⁿ
  • Logarithm Rules: logₐ(mn) = logₐm + logₐn

Geometry Formulas

  • Area of Circle: A = πr²
  • Circumference: C = 2πr
  • Pythagorean Theorem: a² + b² = c²
  • Area of Triangle: A = ½ × b × h
  • Volume of Sphere: V = ⁴⁄₃πr³

Trigonometry Formulas

  • sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
  • sin(2θ) = 2 sinθ cosθ
  • Law of Sines: a/sinA = b/sinB = c/sinC
  • Law of Cosines: c² = a² + b² - 2ab cosC
  • Area of Triangle: ½ ab sinC

Calculus Formulas

  • Derivative Power Rule: d/dx(xⁿ) = nxⁿ⁻¹
  • Product Rule: (fg)' = f'g + fg'
  • Chain Rule: d/dx[f(g(x))] = f'(g(x))g'(x)
  • Fundamental Theorem: ∫ₐᵇ f(x) dx = F(b) - F(a)
  • Integration by Parts: ∫u dv = uv - ∫v du
